Renters And Housing Union

Western Australia Branch

WE are renters, students, the unhoused and workers.

WE are all organising collectively in Western Australia to get a better outcome for all

For too long those without propety in Western Australia have been gouged, exploited, and ignored by the landlord class and their lackys. RAHU WA says ENOUGH!

Together we fight back! The propertyless have power but only when together!
